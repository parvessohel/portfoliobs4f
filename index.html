The stairs seemed endless to the boy who was climbing them. The boy felt so tired as he was climbing them for who knows how long. But he kept climbing. Tonights the night, he said to himself. I have to get to the top tonight by any means necessary.
At last fate smiled upon him from above. He saw a sparkle of beautiful white light illuminating the end of the climbs. The boy hurried as he was wobbling but reminded himself to be patient and steady. There can't be any mistakes now. As he got closer and closer the light got brighter and brighter. After much strugling he reached to the end of the stairs which linked to a room none like any other he ever saw. The room had no walls still it was sourrounded. It had no windows or doors still brightest of lights illuminating it.
In the middle of it there sat a man of great stature on a gold crafted throne. The boy entered into the room.
"Where were you?" he asked.
I have been looking for you for a long time, he added.
The man looked straight into the eyes of the boy piercing through them into his soul and commanded,
Sit down!
The boy looked around. There was nothing to sit on.
He said, can I lie down though? I am much tired.
Suddenly he felt an incredible pain around his neck as if someone with great strength choking the life out of him. He thought to himself. after all this, I am going to die at the door of my destiny?
The pain slowly disapeared and he sat down on the floor of the strange room.
The man talked, if we are going to do this, there will be some ground rules.
There will be no joking around, you cant speek unless spoken to, there will be no swearing, there will be no debating. There are many others which you already know.
So that we are on the same page, tell me what do you want to know.
The boy waited his entire life for this moment contemplating tirelessly for days and nights.
He asked timidly, not the "Who am I?", not the "What is the meaning of life?" but the first question of all,
What am I?
